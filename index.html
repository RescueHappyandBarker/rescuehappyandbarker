Their Story

Happy and Barker are two dogs living in what we call the Barn of Loneliness. They rely on each other for comfort and safety.

Despite everything, they remain gentle and bonded. They deserve a safe place to sleep, kind people, and a real chance — together.

Happy

  • Loyal and gentle
  • Quiet, thoughtful energy
  • Finds comfort staying close to Barker

Barker

  • Protective and calm
  • Watches over Happy
  • Bonded companion — they do best together

They don’t need much.
Just someone who won’t leave them behind.
